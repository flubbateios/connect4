flubb Connect 4

This is a multiplayer Connect 4 implementation but it's fairly customizable. Board sizes up to 100 x 100 are currently allowed, and the amount that you need to connect is customizable. This server has support for free for alls and you can choose whether to end the game after one person has won or not. Furthermore, there is a spectator client and winners of free for all games get redirected there so they can watch. Here are some buttons to get to various parts of the website. gl hf ;)
